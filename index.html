About Scavengers Haven

Scavengers Haven is a banknotes game on Roblox. Buy a generator and place it to start earning cash, save it up, and then prestige to start over.

Features

Scavengers Haven has a variety of classic and original features such as:

- Amethyst to pack your generators

- Fuel to boost your notes per second

And many more to come!
